Privacy Policy for Imposter

Last updated: December 14, 2024

Introduction

Imposter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Player Names: Temporary player names entered during gameplay are stored only in device memory during active game sessions and are not transmitted or stored externally.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Statistics: Win/loss records and gameplay statistics are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard data storage mechanisms.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Browsing history
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information. Your gameplay statistics remain on your device and are not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

If you make in-app purchases, these transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your data.

Analytics

This app does not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your usage patterns or behavior.

Advertising

This app does not display advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Imposter is a party game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, there is no children's data to protect beyond what is already provided by our privacy practices.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not transmit data to external servers, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device passcode/biometric protection
  • iOS data encryption
  • App sandboxing

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You can clear all local app data by uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: imposterapp.privacy@gmail.com

Consent

By using Imposter, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
